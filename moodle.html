Moodle

A project I did (with another student) at my university that included user interviews, surveys and designing a dark mode.

Summary

We wanted to find out (and address) the biggest problems that students have with Moodle, an e-learning platform used by many faculties at the University of Warsaw. We did a survey asking about the problems, conducted semi-structured interviews and later designed functions that the students thought would help them with the interface.

My job in the design process was to design the dark mode and two options in the interface: student list import and course material export. When designing the dark mode I used some of the principle of Material Design.
